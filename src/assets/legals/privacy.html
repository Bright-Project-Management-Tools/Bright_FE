Bright Website Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Bright, the owner and data controller of Bright website, collects, uses, and discloses your personal information when you visit the website.

Personal Information We Collect

We collect two types of information:

  • Device Information: This is automatically collected information about your device, including your web browser, IP address, time zone, and some cookies. This information is used to identify potential abuse and for website usage statistics.
  • Personal Data: This is information you provide during registration, such as your name, email address, and city. This information is used to fulfill agreements and provide website features.

Why We Process Your Data

We prioritize data security and only process minimal user data necessary for website maintenance. Device information is used for abuse detection and website usage statistics.

Your Rights

If you are a European resident, you have the following data rights:

  • The right to be informed
  • The right of access
  • The right to rectification
  • The right to erasure
  • The right to restrict processing
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to object
  • Rights in relation to automated decision-making and profiling

To exercise these rights, please contact us through the contact information below.

Links to Other Websites

Our website may contain links to other websites with different privacy practices. We recommend reviewing their privacy statements.

Information Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no internet transmission is guaranteed secure.

Legal Disclosure

We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights and the safety of others.
